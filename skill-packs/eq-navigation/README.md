# EQ & Navigation Skill Pack

6 skills for navigating human dynamics: difficult conversations, stakeholder influence, meeting debriefs, delegation, energy management, and negotiation.

## Skills (6)

| Skill                  | What It Does                                                                                         |
| ---------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Difficult Conversation | Structures any confrontation, negotiation, or sensitive topic with opening lines and exit strategies |
| Stakeholder Influence  | Maps interests and builds sequenced influence plans to get buy-in from resistant stakeholders        |
| Room Reader            | Post-meeting dynamics debrief: who was aligned, who was resistant, what wasn't said                  |
| Delegation Planner     | Structures what to hand off, to whom, how to brief them, and how to track without micromanaging      |
| Energy Audit           | Identifies energy drains and misallocations across calendar, tasks, and priorities                   |
| Negotiation Prep       | Maps BATNA, ZOPA, opening position, concession plan, and walk-away point                             |

## Install

Append the contents of `INDEX.md` to the Skills Index table in your CLAUDE.md.
