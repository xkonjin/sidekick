# Voice Dictation with Sidekick

Sidekick works great with voice. Dictate instead of typing for faster, more natural interactions.

## Setup

### macOS

1. System Settings > Keyboard > Dictation
2. Turn on Dictation
3. Set shortcut (default: press Fn twice)
4. In any text field, press the shortcut and start talking

### iOS / iPadOS

1. Settings > General > Keyboard > Enable Dictation
2. Tap the microphone icon on any keyboard
3. Works in the Claude mobile app

## Workflows

### Think Out Loud

Dictate a stream of consciousness. Sidekick structures it.

> "Ok so the meeting with the design lead went well, she's on board with the May timeline but wants us to nail down the API spec first. The VP is going to push back on the budget but I think if we show the user research numbers he'll come around. Oh and we need to loop in the engineering lead on the infrastructure thing, that's been blocking us for two weeks now."

Sidekick will extract: action items, decisions, people to follow up with, blockers.

### Voice-Draft Messages

> "Write an email to the engineering lead about the infrastructure migration. Tone should be friendly but firm. We've been waiting two weeks and the rollout can't start without it."

Sidekick matches your communication style automatically.

### Meeting Debrief

Walk out of a meeting and dictate what happened:

> "Just finished the product sync. Big takeaway is we're pushing launch to May instead of April. The research lead presented the user interviews, 60 done so far. The designer showed the new onboarding flow. Action item for me is to update the go-to-market timeline."

Sidekick routes this to the right memory files.

### Status Updates

> "Weekly update: SEO is moving, we got 15 pages indexed up from 5. The migration is still blocked on infrastructure, I'm escalating today. Website rebuild is on track, vendor demo on Friday."

Sidekick formats this against your priorities.

## Tips

- **Don't worry about filler words.** "Um", "uh", "so basically" get filtered naturally.
- **Corrections work mid-sentence.** "The deadline is March... actually April 15th" - Sidekick takes the correction.
- **Punctuation is automatic.** Modern dictation handles periods, commas, and question marks.
- **Say "new line" or "new paragraph"** to control structure if needed.
